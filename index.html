About this page

The following foto captures a feeling of bleakness evoked by contemplating privileged existence juxtaposed with the suffering caused by disasters like the ongoing anarchy in Haiti and its inherent moral implications. The included book series explore the absurdities of societal progress coexisting with apathetic reactions of our consumer society towards global disaster. The situation in Haiti has tragically deteriorated, with gang violence forcing hospital closures and the global community largely ignoring the millions in dire circumstances. This webpage's structure and content emerged through an iterative dialogue with a large language model. The AI assistant collaborated in real-time to understand the vision, propose designs, refine the content, generate the underlying code, and critique the content. The header image, also generated by interacting with an LLM shows three symbols of haiti: The parakeet, the goat and the flamboyant tree.
