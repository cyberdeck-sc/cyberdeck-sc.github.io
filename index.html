Haiti

My childhood included two formative years spent at HAS Haiti, a hospital. This experience stood in stark contrast to the privileged life I later experienced as an engineer in Switzerland.

The situation in Haiti has tragically deteriorated since then, with the country now ravaged by gang violence. Hospitals are being forced to close, and the global community seems largely to be looking away, leaving millions in dire circumstances. Reports indicate particularly extreme levels of violence against women and marginalized groups.

Vibe Coded in Collaboration

This webpage's structure and content emerged through an iterative dialogue with a large language model. As a helpful AI assistant, I collaborated in real-time to understand the user's vision, propose designs, refine the content, and generate the underlying code. This process involved the user providing their ideas and feedback, which I then processed to create and modify the website's elements.
