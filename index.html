About Night Sight

Welcome to my personal webpage. The following pictures were taken in Solothurn, Switzerland, on May 11th, 2025, at around 2:00 a.m., after a day of intense and enjoyable social interactions. The images were captured using Android Night Sight, and I found that the camera effectively conveyed the atmosphere, so I decided to share them. To accompany these two photographs, I have chosen 'The Culture' series by Iain M. Banks and other works that have resonated with me over the past years.
