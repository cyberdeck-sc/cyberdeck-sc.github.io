Echoes of HAS

The linked book series explore the absurdities of societal progress coexisting with apathetic reactions of our consumer society towards global disaster. The situation in Haiti has tragically deteriorated, with gang violence forcing hospital closures and the global community largely ignoring the millions in dire circumstances. This webpage's structure, the image and the content emerged through an iterative dialogue with a large language model. The AI assistant collaborated in real-time to understand the vision, propose designs, refine the content, generate the underlying code, and critique the content.
